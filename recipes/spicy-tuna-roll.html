Spicy Tuna Roll Recipe

Description

A delicious recipe for a spicy tuna roll. Easy to follow and make homeade, cheap sushi of your own.

Ingredients

  • Sushi rice
  • Bamboo mat
  • Nori
  • Tuna
  • Sesame oil
  • Mayo
  • Sriracha

Directions

  1. Prepare rice and let cool
  2. Chop tuna into small pieces, add to bowl
  3. Add mayo, sesame oil, sriracha (your choice of additional spices)
  4. Place bamboo mat on table
  5. Lay nori sheet on mat
  6. Scoop one cup of rice onto nori sheet and spread
  7. Add thin layer of tuna to center of spread out rice
  8. Roll with bamboo matt
  9. Cut into 6 pieces
